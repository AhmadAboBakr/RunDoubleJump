Objective

This event is targeting all Arab-Indie Game Developers as well as gamers. The gamers will discover the games produced by Indie developers through Indie Games Exhibit. This experience will make them help one another in completing the missing aspects of every game. This will help in promoting Indie development in MENA region and hence promoting its Gaming Industry.

Target Audiance

  • Game Developers

    one of the main attractions of the event is for game developers to come and showcase thier work, this will help give them exposure and further improve the industry

  • Gamers

    Gamers are welcomed to try the gamed produced by arab developers, give them feed back and have a grasp of the stage the industry is at.

  • Students

    We will have session provided by various speakers about the game development industry. you will learn where to get started in your career as an indie game developer and what consider when making your first game
